📖 Crop Encyclopedia

      
    

    

    

      
🎮 Available Functions

      

        plant("crop", x, y) - Plant a crop at position (x, y)

        water(x, y) - Water the crop (cost: 2g)

        fertilize(x, y) - Fertilize (cost: 3g)

        harvest(x, y) - Harvest a mature crop

        wait(seconds) - Wait for time to pass

        is_mature(x, y) - Returns True/False

        get_weather() - Get current weather

        get_status(x, y) - Get crop status at (x, y)

        clear() - Clear the entire field

        print(value) - Print to console

        sell(x, y) - Sell at market price

        get_price("crop") - Get current market price

        get_market() - Get all market prices

        get_season() - Get current season

        get_gold() - Get your gold amount

        get_time() - Get farm time

        get_all_mature() - List all mature crop positions

        get_all_planted() - List all planted crops

        count_crops() - Count planted crops

        has_pest(x, y) - Check for pest

        remove_pest(x, y) - Remove pest (cost: 5g)

        get_pests() - List all pests
